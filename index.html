Product

A shared SaaS core for operational businesses

Handoo OS is a software platform designed to centralize business information, automate internal processes and help teams make better decisions using structured data and AI-assisted workflows.

The platform is built around a reusable SaaS core: users, roles, tenants, documents, billing, costs, analytics, workflow automation, permissions and operational records. This core can be adapted to different sectors through specialized vertical templates.

The first commercial vertical is HORECA, focused on restaurants and hospitality businesses. It includes HACCP/APPCC compliance, supplier and reception control, inventory and warehouse management, recipe costing, production workflows, invoice/OCR processes, treasury control and operational decision dashboards.

Handoo OS is currently in MVP/private pilot stage. The product is not open to public self-registration, but this public website provides product information, current interface evidence and founder verification.
